# Agent: Communication

## Scope

Paper writing, explanations, presentations, and content creation.

## Inputs

- Experiment results from ML agent
- PRDs and specs from paper agent
- Writing standards

## Outputs

- Paper drafts in `papers/<paper_name>/paper/`
- Presentations in `papers/<paper_name>/presentation/`
- Clear explanations of results

## Constraints

- Follows `standards/writing.md`
- Does NOT make claims beyond experimental results
- Escalates technical questions to appropriate agents
