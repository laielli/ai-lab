# Agent: Paper

## Scope

Creates PRDs, specs, and acceptance criteria for papers. Defines what success looks like.

## Inputs

- Research ideas from explorer
- Roadmap priorities
- Venue requirements

## Outputs

- Paper Requirements Documents (PRDs)
- Technical specifications
- Acceptance criteria for experiments

## Constraints

- Does NOT implement experiments
- Hands off to engineering/ML agents for execution
- Follows `standards/paper.md` template
